The Enpod

What is the Enpod? Enpod is a design the Sköldpaddor team came up with to solve one of the biggest issue in 21st century: Spinal Issues.
Here is our engineering process:

Ask

We asked a series of questions about what are people’s greatest health concerns using the social media “Instagram”.

Research

During the research phase, we looked up for 21st century health concerns online; we found information regarding different kinds of chairs that has already been invented so our product won’t overlap.

Imagine

Due to the lack of time, each team member only created 1 design to try to solve the problem.

Plan

During the process of planning we discussed about what to use for the materials of the chair, so we can keep it in our budget.

Create

During the crate phase, we sent 2 members of our team to go to the tech cube after asking for permission from the HQ to get our materials.

Test

For testing, we let our heaviest weighted person (80 kg) to test if the chair breaks, to make sure that most people won’t be in trouble if they use it.

Improve

nstead of dense sponge/normal seat for seating, we used the bike seating to make our chair more versatile when people are traveling on a bike. In the future, we could add more adjustable lengths for taller people and stand-sitting.
